Private Pilot Training

Instrument and Commercial Training

Clouds? Weather? Enhance your skills and experience level. We can plan a course of instrument training that works for you. We offer both part 61 or 141 instrument training based on your experience level and what makes the most sense for you.

The Sanford Seacoast is a great instrument training location! And if the weather is just too awful to fly then we can always utilize our AATD Redbird simulator to keep your instrument progress going.

Our ATTD Redbird simulator can help you attune your skills on the ground and for when weather doesn’t allow for airplane use.

Advancing your flying skills with a commercial license creates open doors in an aviation career.

With both Part 61 and 141 training programs available, you are able to choose which path works best for you. Our complex and technically advanced aircraft will give you the needed experience to meet federal requirements.

Backcountry Experience Training

Are you looking to expand your light aircraft experiences? In our best New England summer weather, learn how to get in and out of small grass and dirt strips near mountains and along the coast. No previous experience required.

Tailwheel Training

Some pilots really love to fly tailwheel airplanes and for them we have a Citabria. Ours was built in 2004, though the design dates back to Grandpa’s time. It is truly “stick and rudder” flying, with fantastic visibility.
