Aircraft Maintenance

Our experienced mechanics can help give you the peace of mind that comes from knowing that your aircraft is in top notch condition and has been inspected thoroughly and professionally.

The maintenance staff at Southern Maine Aviation loves aviation and flying as much as you do, and safety is our number one priority. We keep our clients informed and included throughout the maintenance process or project for your aircraft.

Our maintenance staff has thousands of hours of experience on almost all General Aviation aircraft, helicopters, experimental and light sport aircraft, plus numerous corporate jets and turbine engines. Maintenance and repair services include:

  • Annual inspections
  • Pre-purchase inspections
  • General preventive maintenance and repairs
  • Dynamic propeller and rotor system balancing
  • Engine changes
  • GPS database updates
  • Aircraft weighing
  • Modifications and STC installations

Our mechanics are factory trained and authorized for Rotax engine maintenance and we are an official service center for Robinson Helicopters and Diamond Aircraft.

Please call Brian Westover (207-324-8919) to discuss details for your annual inspection or other repair work.

Annual inspection prices are based on the aircraft, with any necessary repairs charged at the shop hourly labor rate.

The shop rate is:
$90/hour for fixed-wing aircraft
$95/hour for helicopters
$100/hour for turbine equipment
