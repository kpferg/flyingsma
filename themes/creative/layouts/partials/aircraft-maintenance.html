Aircraft Maintenance

Our award-winning mechanics can help give you the peace of mind that comes from knowing that your aircraft is in top notch condition and has been inspected thoroughly and professionally.

The maintenance staff at Southern Maine Aviation cares about you and your safety. We take great pride in our work and consider each customer as friends and family. We also try to make sure you are always informed along the way and included as part of the maintenance process for your aircraft.

Our maintenance staff has thousands of hours of experience on almost all General Aviation aircraft, helicopters, experimental and light sport aircraft, plus numerous corporate jets and turbine engines. Maintenance and repair services include:
Annual inspections
Pre-purchase inspections
General preventive maintenance and repairs
Dynamic propeller and rotor system balancing
Engine changes
GPS database updates
Aircraft weighing
Modifications and STC installations

Our mechanics are factory trained and authorized for Rotax engine maintenance and we are an official service center for Robinson Helicopters and Diamond Aircraft.

Please call to discuss details for your annual inspection or other repair work.
