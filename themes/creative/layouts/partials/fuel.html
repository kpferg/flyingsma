Fuel

Southern Maine Aviation has full service Jet A and 100LL during business hours, plus self service for Jet A, Avgas, and Mogas at all hours. The self-serve pumps for Avgas and Jet A are on the main ramp on the northeast side. The self-serve Mogas is on the west side next to the Big Blue Hangar on taxiway C.

After-hours services are available upon request (call 207-651-9308).
